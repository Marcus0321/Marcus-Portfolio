About Me

I’m Marcus Mangubat, a 3rd-year BS Information Technology student at City College of Calamba. I’m known for being highly disciplined, ambitious, and excellent at time management, always delivering quality work while balancing academics and personal projects. What drives me the most is my passion for continuous growth — I’m constantly learning new skills, tools, and technologies to evolve both creatively and technically. I love turning ideas into beautiful, functional digital products that solve real problems.

As a designer-developer, I bring together strong creative and technical abilities. In design, I’m skilled in graphic design, Adobe Photoshop, Canva (especially for branding kits, social media, and presentations), and Figma for UI/UX design, wireframing, and prototyping. On the development side, I’m proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, and database management, enabling me to build responsive, full-stack web solutions from scratch. I’m eager to apply this unique blend of skills in internships, freelance projects, or full-time roles where I can keep learning, contribute meaningfully, and grow alongside a talented team.
